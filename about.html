Making a difference

One meal at a time

In the US alone, it is estimated that approximately 119 billion pounds of food are wasted every year. We created this application with the intent to encourage people to use ingrediants before they expire. Not only will you be cutting down on food waste but you can save some money too!

We are a team of Jr Developers in training at Denver University Coding Boot Camp. Our mission is to deliver exceptional applications with an emphisis on user experience and practicality.
