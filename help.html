What's In Your Fridge?

How do I use this app?

First, navigate to the "Get Started" link on the home page. They you will see a list of categories to choose from. Select your items. Once your items are selected, they will appear in a list at the bottom of the "Get Started" tab. From there, youll click the "Get Recipes" link and it will redirect you to a new window with reccomended recipes based on your selection.

Create an account and save your recipe by dragging and dropping the recipe into your account.
